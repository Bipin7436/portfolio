Who Are You?

Who Are You? is a Personality Developement Website. In general there are about 16 personalites in the world, every person on the earth will fall among one of these personalities. This website asks questions related to personality of users. There are around 32 questions with 5 choices each. Based on the option choosen our algorithm will detect the type of personality that he/she has.
Internally this website uses the famous MBTI (Myers-Brigs Type Indicator) for calculating the absolute personalities of the people. The 16 personalities are INFP, ISTJ, ISTP, ISFJ, ISFP, INFJ, INTJ, INTP, ESTP, ESTJ, ESFP, ESFJ,ENFP, ENFJ, ENTP, ENTJ.. where INFP is "The Idealistic", ISTJ is "The Inspector", ISTP is "The Crafstman", ISFJ is "The Nurturer", ISFP is "The Composer", INFJ is "The Counselor", INTJ is "The mastermind", INTP is "The Thinker", ESTP is "The Doer", ESTJ is "The Supervisior", ESFP is "The Performer", ESFJ is "The Provider", ENFP is "The Champion", ENFJ is "The Giver", ENTP is "The Visionary", and ENTJ is "The Commander".

Chitti-The Bot

Chitti-The Bot is a chatbot, which is being developed for the agricultural usage. It can be easily used by every farmer by simply tapping on the options given below on the screen. Chitti is very easy to use, because it supports different languages like English, Hindi, Telugu,..etc With the help of Chitti, a farmer can get all the information related to the crops, like in which season it would be better for rice,wheat,.., how much water a crop will use, when it will be harvested..etc, Chitti also contains a feature, which allows farmers to check where are the near-by fertilizers and seeds shops, so that he/she can know the best price of them. It also give information about how to get profit in the market for a specific crop.
